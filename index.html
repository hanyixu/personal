I am a junior at Syracuse University Newhouse School of Public Communications, majoring in Advertising and minoring in Computer Engineering. While focusing learning real-world advertising skills, I am very interested in data analyzing and front-end (website) development.

Things to say...

I chose a career in advertising because I enjoy the combination of social science and math. I find the research and analysis involved in advertising fascinating, and I love uncovering insights into consumer behavior. Understanding how people think and what drives their decisions is an important aspect of advertising, and it's a challenge that I enjoy taking on. I also appreciate the power of storytelling in public communications and the ability to create impactful productions that resonate with audiences. For me, advertising is a dynamic and exciting field that combines my passions for research, analysis, and creative storytelling.

Studying advertising at Newhouse, Syracuse University was transformative for me. It developed my data skills, broadened my perspective, and developed my leadership. As a student, I took on leadership roles, developed team skills and became culturally competent. Studying at Newhouse gave me the tools to succeed in the industry and as a person.
