I am a junior at Syracuse University Newhouse School of Public Communications, majoring in Advertising and minoring in Computer Engineering. While focusing learning real-world advertising skills, I am very interested in data analyzing and software development.

Things to say...

Advertising is an art, not only in production and publicity, but more in researching product personality, researching consumer needs, formulating and implementing creative strategies. Advertising involves various skills such as ethnography, quantitative research, and data analysis, rather than just designing a pretty poster. Studying advertising at Newhouse at Syracuse University has given me data collection and processing skills, as well as an understanding of world dynamics and the humanities of different places. I got a chance to see the world from a different perspective.
